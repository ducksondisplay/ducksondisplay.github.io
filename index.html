About the Show

Ducks on Display is Stevens Tech FIRST EVER, large scale, talent variety show open to any and all types of talent. Your peers will aim to entertain and you, as the audience will get the chance to vote for your favorites! See below for the featured performers in this year's second annual Ducks on Display taking place in Debaun Auditorium on Wednesday, February 20 @ 9 PM!

Performers

Kelly McGowan

I'm a senior Visual Arts and Technology major, and the president of Poetry Club. I've been writing poetry since high school, and have grown to love it even more during my college years. I combine my love for poetry with other art mediums, such as video, collage, and graphic design, in an attempt to explore the visual side to poetry.

Emily Ng

I've been singing since I was 5 years old. I started by singing along to my sister playing piano and then grew to play music for myself. I am very passionate about music and it would be my dream profession.

Eleni

Hi! My name is Eleni and I'm a 2/5 Software Engineering major. I've been singing for fun since I was little, I did a lot of theatre in high school, and I love making people laugh! I will be singing a comedic theatrical piece called "Taylor the Latte Boy". My goal is to put a smile on your face...so fingers crossed!

Jordan Torres

Jordan has been singing and playing piano ever since she was young and started performing when she was 10. Music has been a huge part of her life and she loves what she does. Today she'll be performing one of her favorite songs, Creep, by Radiohead.

Erwiin

Erwiin is an independent artist/producer from Monmouth County, NJ. The young artist strives to create vivid stories through his music, with the help of frequent collaborator Justin Kroeger. While his lyrics are portrayed through somber melodies, Erwiin’s ambient production allows them to shine through within their own world. Erwiin is currently on major streaming platforms such as Spotify and AppleMusic.

Mia & Carter

We are playing de Bériot's Duo for 2 violins No. 12 op. 87 and have both been playing violin for over 10 years. We are both in the Stevens Orchestra and are passionate about playing music. Playing duets is fun--we played a Leclair Sonata for 2 violins for the orchestra concert this past fall.

Jordan & Garrett

We are a duet on-the-rise. Our debut performance at Open Mic Night brought us together as a group, and our ambitions have never been higher.

Some Strings Attached

First, there will be a short introduction of modern yoyoing. Then, music will begin to play, and choreographed yoyo routine will be performed to the music.

Megha & Cameron

We are tap dancers from Flemington, NJ and have been dancing for as long as we can remember! The act we will be performing is a tap duet to Khalid's "American Teen". Tap dancing is an amazing art form and we hope you enjoy it!

Floctaves

Floctaves is the newest a cappella group on campus, founded three semesters ago in Fall 2017 by a group of freshmen, now sophomores. They met during performing arts pre-orientation and shared their clear love of music through small impromptu jam sessions which later evolved into full-scale rehearsals twice a week. Since then, they have grown a lot and expanded to welcome lots of great new singers and friends!

TECHnique

Technique is made up of dancers with a range of experience. We teach classes, take classes, and perform together to share what we love doing.

Nick & Jess

We’re just two friends who really wanted to perform together. When we heard there was a chance we jumped at the opportunity. We’re both extremely excited to have a go at it.
